Rooms


Standard Rooms

Relax in our Standard Room, featuring modern amenities and cozy furnishings for a comfortable stay. Enjoy a restful night's sleep in either a king or twin bed setup, with the option for additional conveniences like a work desk and complimentary toiletries.

Deluxe Rooms

Experience tranquility in our Deluxe Room, offering serene garden and city views. Choose between a king or twin bed, and enjoy amenities such as a work desk, safety deposit box, and luxurious bath essentials. Request a smoke-free room for added comfort, subject to availability.

Suite Rooms

Indulge in luxury with our Suite Room, offering spacious elegance and exquisite amenities. Experience ultimate comfort in a separate living area, plush bedding, and a well-appointed bathroom with premium toiletries.
